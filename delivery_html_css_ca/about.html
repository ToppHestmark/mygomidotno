About Us

A City bike tour based in Oslo. We are three student who started this company as a school project. With focus on enviroment and tourism we came up with an idea to offer our visitors to have an alternative and eco-friendly way to explore the city. We offer group tour with a local guide who will show you the best spot of the city.
